Today's Practice NEET / Class 12 Level

Which of the following has the highest pH?

A. 0.01 M HCl

B. 0.01 M NaOH

C. 0.1 M CH3COOH

D. 0.1 M NH4Cl

Answer: B. 0.01 M NaOH

Sodium hydroxide is a strong base and provides the highest concentration of OH- ions among the given options, so it has the highest pH.
